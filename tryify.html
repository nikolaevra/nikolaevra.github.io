Tryify @deCode Hackathon

Github:

Created during deCODE Waterloo in partnership with Shopify. A WebApp that changes the way people shop online and enables a unified store for millions of products tryify.shop. Now anyone can start an online stop even if your inventory consists only of one product! Simply upload your product and it will be added to an infinite store carousel with an ability to like your favorite items and checkout with Shopify .
